Tether (USDT)
Definition:
Tether is a stablecoin, meaning its value is pegged to a stable asset (such as the US Dollar) to reduce the volatility typically seen with cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum. 1 USDT is typically worth around 1 USD, and Tether is commonly used as a means of transferring value or as a stable store of value in the crypto space.
